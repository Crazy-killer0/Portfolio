Introduction

You have been contacted by the desperate family of Ellen Gravist, who have had no contact with her for several weeks. At odds with her nearest and dearest, she had given little news but was showing signs of life.

They are unable to give you any further details about the circumstances, which explains why the police have not considered the disappearance to be a cause for concern…

The attached PDF file contains the little information the family have been able to give you.

After telling them that you will not be able to give them any information without Ellen’s permission (if you find her), but that you will inform her of their concerns, you accept the mission.
